Welcome to Meltdown

Meltdown is a game of stress and speed. With only thirty minutes to escape before the facility is vaporized, all players will be rolling their dice at the same time in order to progress through the facility and reach the teleporter. Everyone will be constantly battling for control, and if you can hold your ground for 5 minutes while the teleporter charges, you will escape, leaving the others to their doomed fate.

So What Happened?

The scientists had a 'bring your pet to work' day and, as expected, it went horribly wrong. One of the pets destroyed a crucial component that was maintaining the nuclear reactor!

Bad Icarus!

Only One Will Escape

The teleporter is in the center of the facility, and only has enough charge left for a single person. Hope you are ready to fight, because that teleporter is your only way out. It's time to test your luck. Oh ya... be careful. There is also a rat army. Thanks Rattus...

Some Unforeseen Changes

This facility and its occupants had questionable traits to begin with, but thanks to the initial explosion, these scientists now find themselves in an especially deadly situation. The creatures that were once contained in their labs have escaped, and the chemicals that were once carefully isolated are now mixing. Many obstacles now stand in the way of a successful escape.

  • Biology Lab
  • Botany Lab
  • Physics Lab
  • Hazard Lab
  • Hydrology Lab
  • Solar Lab
