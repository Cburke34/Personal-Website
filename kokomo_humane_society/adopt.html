What's Included?

  • Microchip and registration
  • 30-day gift of pet health insurance
  • 1 or 2 pound bag of Hill's Science Diet pet food
  • Leash (dogs)
  • Free "Get your pet to a vet" wellness visit courtesy of six of our local vets.
  • And the most important part...
    You will receive the unconditional love of a wonderful animal AND a second chance for the adopted animal.

Adoption fees are donations to the Kokomo Humane Society, and are refundable only with the approval of the Executive Director.

Please note that certain animals have special fees associated with them.

We accept cash or credit only for the adoption fee. No Checks allowed.

To speed up the adoption process you can download our adoption application. Please present your completed application in person or fax it to (765) 454-0082. Mailed applications are not accepted.

We welcome your interest in our animals, and it is our policy to offer "hold service" when possible. Animals can be held for 24 hours with a $25 non-refundable deposit which is applicable towards the adoption fee. After 48 hours, the animal will be placed back up for adoption.

If you are traveling in the hopes of adopting one of our pets, please call ahead to ensure the animal you wish to adopt has not already been adopted. If you have questions feel free to email us at: adoption@kokomohumane.org
